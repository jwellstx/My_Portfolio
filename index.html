About Me

Hi, my name is Justin Wells! I was born and raised in the great state of Texas, in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. In 2007, I graduated from Grapevine High School and began to persue my career interesets in computers and programming. I Attended the University of Texas at Austin, where I graduated in 2011 with a Bachelors in Electrical and Computer Engineering Degree. Since then I have worked with Intel Corporation as a Product Development Engineering doing Manufacturing and Validation, maintaining software tools. I am currently in a full stack web development coarse to broaden my programming skills and learn more about web design which has interested me for some time now. I enjoy writing new scripts and code and the ideas the planning and problem solving that goes into designing new solutions.

Some of my hobbies include playing with my animals which include a Boxer dog and two horses. I also enjoy riding motorcycle and playing videogames. Once a nice summer day, I enjoy having a cold beer and one of the many local breweries out where I live in Dripping Springs, Texas.
