About Me

Hi, my name is Justin Wells! I was born and raised in the great state of Texas, in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. In 2007, I graduated from Grapevine High School and began to persue my career with computers and programming. I attended the University of Texas at Austin, where I graduated in 2011 with a Bachelors Degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Since then, I have worked with Intel Corporation as a Product Development Engineer doing Manufacturing, Validation, and maintaining software tools. I am currently in a full stack web development course to broaden my programming skills and enhance my knowledge of web design. I am always looking for opportunities to expand my skill set and try new challenges. I enjoy problem solving and designing new solutions by writing new scripts and code. It is fullfilling to take an idea and create code to bring the idea to life.

My hobbies include playing with my animals- which include a super cute Boxer dog and two horses. I also enjoy riding motorcycles and playing videogames. On a nice summer day, I enjoy having a cold beer at one of the many local breweries out near my home in Dripping Springs, Texas.
